Inspire others

Sharing knowledge has always been hard. There is a ton of ways of sharing information like docs and notes, and yet we always end up with too many of them.

How can you actually transfer the important stuff of what you've learned to someone else? Readsmart lets you do just that. Share with others all of what you have learned about a specific topic or theme fast and easy in a holistic way.

Build a knowledge base that others can access to. Help them save time to get answers quickly.

Learn smarter, not harder

Remembering is hard. Our brains are designed to forget by default. In order to overcome the “forgetting curve”, we need to convince our brains that the information matters.

Readsmart helps you recap your most important highlights and notes by reminding you when to review them. This process is based on a study technique called Spaced Repetition, that places increased intervals of time before reviewing the information you're trying to learn. Every time you review them, you are more likely to discover new insights about a specific topic.

This how we make the best use of your highlights, otherwise what’s the point of taking them in the first place?
